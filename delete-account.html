Account & Data Deletion Request

App Name: Smart Kisaan / Bhumi Mitra (com.bhumimitra)

Developer Entity: Guiltees

We respect your privacy and give you full control over your data. If you wish to delete your account and all associated personal data (including land records, photos, and profile information) from the Smart Kisaan app, you can do so by following the instructions below.

How to request data deletion:

  1. Inside the App: Open the Smart Kisaan app, navigate to your Profile or Settings page, and tap on "Delete Account". This will instantly remove your data from our servers.
  2. Via Email: If you no longer have access to the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us directly.

    Please send an email to bhumimitraapp@gmail.com from the email address or phone number associated with your account.
    Subject: Account Deletion Request - Smart Kisaan
    Body: Please include your registered phone number so we can identify and securely delete your account.

Note: Data deletion requests sent via email will be processed within 30 days in compliance with Google Play Data Safety policies. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered.

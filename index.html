This Privacy Policy explains how the Bhumi Mitra Team ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use our Android applications.

7 Advertising (Google AdMob)

We display ads through Google AdMob to keep the app free. Ad formats used:

  • Banner Ads — small ads at top/bottom of screens.
  • Interstitial Ads — full-screen ads at natural transitions.
  • Rewarded Ads — optional ads for in-app rewards.

Control Ad Personalization

  • Device: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • Online: Google Ad Settings
  • You can reset your Advertising ID at any time via device settings.
ℹ️ Opting out of personalized ads will not remove ads — you will still see non-personalized advertisements.

8 Data Storage & Security

Data is stored on Google Firebase (Google Cloud Platform) servers. Local preferences are stored on-device via Shared Preferences.

  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data in transit.
  • Firebase Security Rules restrict access to authorized users.
  • OTP-based auth — no passwords stored.
⚠️ No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

11 India DPDP Act 2023 Compliance

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023:

  • Lawful Purpose: Data collected only for purposes described in this policy.
  • Consent: Your informed consent is obtained before data collection. You are a Data Principal under the DPDP Act.
  • Data Minimization: Only necessary data is collected.
  • Accuracy: You can update your data within the app.
  • Storage Limitation: Data retained only as long as necessary.
  • Data Principal Rights: Access, correct, erase data, and nominate another person for rights exercise.
  • Grievance Redressal: Email bhumimitraapp@gmail.com — responded within prescribed timeframes.
  • Data Fiduciary: Bhumi Mitra Team acts as Data Fiduciary under the DPDP Act.

13 Children's Privacy

Our apps are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact bhumimitraapp@gmail.com — we will delete it promptly.

14 Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained until you request deletion.
  • Land records: Retained until deleted by you or upon account deletion.

  • Local preferences: Cleared on app uninstall or data clear.

Deletion requests are processed within 30 days.
